Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers.

What is the TOMAR F11 Smartpen?

The TOMAR F11 is an AI-powered smartpen that transforms your handwritten notes into editable digital text in real time. It combines the traditional feel of pen and paper with the convenience and power of digital note-taking.

How does the TOMAR F11 work?

The F11 uses a built-in micro-camera and advanced AI to capture your handwriting. As you write, the pen tracks your pen strokes and instantly converts them into digital text, which is then synced to the TOMAR app on your smartphone or tablet.

What languages does the TOMAR F11 support?

The TOMAR F11's handwriting recognition technology supports an impressive 66 languages, making it a versatile tool for global users.

Can I use the TOMAR F11 offline?

Yes, the F11 has a generous offline storage capacity of up to 800 pages. Once you reconnect to the internet, your notes will automatically sync to the cloud.
